Surf Snowdonia

Surf Snowdonia is an artificial surfing place, supplied with water from mountains in Snowdonia that has been acclaimed as one of the main tourist attraction in North Wales. You can have surf lessons, or you can freely surf. I have been to the cafe before with my family, but it was hailing and was too windy so we weren't allowed to surf.
