North Wales

Most years in the easter holidays, i go to North Wales with my family. We stay near Snowdonia and Conwy and do lots of things, including sleeping till midday and going for long, forced walks. In the past i have done activities such as wind surfing, ziplining and paintballing there, but this time, i have been told by my parents that we are doing surfing at surf snowdonia and white water rafting at the river dee. We also go to the annual easter egg hunt at Bodnant Gardens which is Fun. I will tell you about what i hope to do this year.
